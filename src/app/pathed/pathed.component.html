PATHeD

The project aims to provide Irish Citizens with access to their healthcare data via a mobile application called eHealthPass.

The eHealthPass App aims to enable seamless cross-border care and securely exchange patient information in the form of Patient Summaries (a snapshot of the patient's healthcare data) between the Irish healthcare infrastructure and the eHealthPass mobile application following the Cross-Border Directive 2011/24/EU The exchange of data will be via the MyHealth@EU services. In doing so, it will enhance the healthcare encounter and reduce the frequency of medical errors by providing quick access to patient health information while abroad.

For further information on eHealthPass, please see the PATHeD EU HealthPass
