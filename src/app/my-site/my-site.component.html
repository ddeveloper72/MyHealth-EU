MyHealth@EU

About us:

MyHealth@EU is a team of IT professionals dedicated to exchanging health data between Ireland and EU member states. As part of Irelandâ€™s commitment to the EU, Ireland is mandated to exchange health data with our European peers to enable digital access to health data for health professionals and citizens. We work with our colleagues in the National Shared Care Records to develop and exchange structured, coded data, across the European Health Data Space.

Our people:

The people in the MyHealth@EU team come from many different career entry points and each is highly skilled and well-educated. We are also part of National Shared Care Records.

Our team is comprised of a diverse range of highly experienced IT professionals across both public and private sectors. We specialise in interoperability and design, data integration, software testing, enterprise & technical architecture, and development of standards to exchange healthcare data.

MyHealth@EU - What we do:

The projects that we work on, enable and deliver change. We focus on providing interoperability between different systems that will improve access to healthcare for Irish citizens travelling abroad in Europe as well as for EU citizens visiting Ireland.

Projects we are involved in include;

  • MyHealth@EU
  • Electronic Health Record (EHR)
  • Partnerships and Consortiums
    • PATHeD
    • xShare

Our Mission Statement:

To bring about change by advancing European healthcare through interoperability, which will provide better access to healthcare for citizens.
