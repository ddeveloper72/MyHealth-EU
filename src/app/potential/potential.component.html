Potential For European Digital Identity

Potential is a European Digital identity Initiative which focuses on;

  • Digital Transformation - increasing digitalization of our world and how our daily lives are becoming more interconnected.
  • Online Access - democratization of online access and usage.
  • Digital Identity - Emphasizes the importance of having a secure, authentic, and verifiable digital identity.
  • eIDAS 2 Regulation - The European Commission's eIDAS 2 regulation aims to fully realize the vision of a cross-border European Digital Identity Wallet, ensuring user-centricity and full protection of personal data.
To learn more about Potential, please refer to the European Digital Identity Wallet at Potential - For European Digital Identity (digital-identity-wallet.eu)

This is a project Co-funded by the European Union.
